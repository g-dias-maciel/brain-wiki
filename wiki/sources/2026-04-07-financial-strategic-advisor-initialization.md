## Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive financial strategy centered around a "Buy, Borrow, Die" (Lombard Loan) approach using margin lending against a concentrated tech ETF portfolio. The strategy aims to build wealth while avoiding capital gains taxes and preparing for global location independence.

## Key Components

### 1. Personal Financial Profile
- **Location**: Helmstedt, Germany
- **Residency**: German resident for >6 years
- **Income**: €3,328.32/month (German salary) + €500-800/month (Brazilian business)
- **Monthly Surplus**: ~€530 from German salary
- **Cash Buffer**: €1,000-1,500 emergency fund

### 2. The Margin Engine Strategy
- **Broker**: Scalable Capital (PRIME+ Tier, Baader Bank)
- **Margin Rate**: 3.00%-4.24% p.a.
- **Core Asset**: 100% QDVE (iShares S&P 500 Information Technology Sector UCITS ETF)
- **Strategy**: Leverage tech sector growth (~15-16% p.a.) against margin cost (~4% p.a.)

### 3. Operational Rules
- **Buy (Predator Mode)**: Invest 100% monthly surplus into QDVE; use margin to buy dips up to 35% LTV
- **Borrow**: Use margin for expenses instead of selling shares
- **Payback (ATH Rule)**: Pay down margin debt when QDVE is at/near all-time highs

### 4. Risk Management Framework
- **Target LTV**: 35% (survives ~53% market crash)
- **Interest Freeze (LTV >45%)**: Pay interest in cash only
- **Cash Shield (LTV >55%)**: Inject €1,500 emergency buffer
- **Nuclear Option (LTV >60%)**: Preemptively sell 10% of portfolio

### 5. Short-Term Projects (2026)
- **Italian Passport**: €116 via consulate
- **Driver's License**: ~€930 for German conversion (B197 automatic) by August 2026
- **Funding**: Brazilian income only (protects margin engine)

### 6. Long-Term Global Exit Plan
- **German Exit Tax**: Avoid €500,000 unrealized gains threshold
- **Migration Plan**:
  1. Grow portfolio to ~€400,000 in Scalable Capital
  2. Open IBKR account before leaving Germany
  3. FOP transfer QDVE shares to IBKR
  4. Deregister from Germany before €500k threshold
  5. Continue margin strategy globally via IBKR
