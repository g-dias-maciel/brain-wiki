## Summary

This document outlines a refined version of the Margin Engine strategy with specific focus on transitioning from German employment to B2B contracting via a Wyoming LLC. It combines the leveraged investment approach with career optimization and advanced tax structuring.

## Key Evolution from Previous Strategy

### 1. Career Transition
- **Current**: PHP Fullstack Developer (employed)
- **Target**: B2B Contractor via Wyoming LLC
- **Revenue**: €75,000 p.a. (€6,250/month)
- **Rationale**: More efficient than €70k gross salary due to eliminated employer social contributions (~25-30%)

### 2. Enhanced Account Structure
- **Broker**: Interactive Brokers (IBKR) - planned migration from Scalable Capital
- **Structure**: Two sub-accounts under Wyoming LLC:
  - **Account A (Predator Core)**: Wealth growth, dip buying (margin interest tax-deductible)
  - **Account B (Life Bridge)**: Personal liquidity, rent funding (margin interest non-deductible)

### 3. Refined Financial Numbers
- **Monthly Expenses**: €3,300 (rent, bills, life)
- **Investment Surplus**: €2,117-2,950/month (depending on margin usage)
- **Life Funding**: Borrow €3,300/month from Account B instead of selling shares

## The "Daily Predator" Framework

### Buy Rules (Enhanced)
- **Dip (Predator Mode)**: Use margin to buy at 10%, 15%, 20% market drops
- **ATH Pause**: No margin buys when within 3% of all-time highs
- **LTV Ceiling**: Stop discretionary buying at 35% LTV

### Borrow Rules (Lombard Bridge)
- **Never Sell**: Fund life through borrowing, not share sales
- **Monthly Draw**: €3,300 from Account B for expenses
- **Growth Spread**: 16-20% asset growth vs. 3-5% margin cost

### Defense Rules (Updated)
- **LTV 45%**: Interest freeze - pay interest in cash only
- **LTV 55%**: Cash shield - inject €1,300+ buffer
- **LTV 60%**: Nuclear option - sell 10% preemptively

## Legal & Tax Optimization (Germany 2026)

### V0050 Exemption
- **Law**: § 6 Abs. 1a SGB VI
- **Benefit**: 3-year exemption from German Social Security (pension insurance)
- **Condition**: Working for single main client as contractor

### VAT Strategy
- **Wyoming LLC**: Bills German GmbHs without VAT
- **Reverse Charge**: Client liable for VAT locally (Art. 196 EU VAT Directive)
- **Efficiency**: Eliminates VAT complexity for international contractor

```
Employer Cost for €70k Gross Employee:
- Salary: €70,000
- Employer Social Contributions: ~€17,500-21,000 (25-30%)
- Total: ~€87,500-91,000

Employer Cost for €75k B2B Contractor:

```
