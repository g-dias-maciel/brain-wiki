## Summary

This document describes a Brazilian marketing agency focused on tattoo artists, currently operating under a mother's MEI (Microempreendedor Individual) structure. The business generates R$8,500 in monthly recurring revenue (MRR) from 5 clients, with Gabriel handling business operations and Daniel managing clients and ads.

## Key Business Details

### Core Operations
- **Focus**: Digital marketing for tattoo artists
- **Location**: Brazil
- **Team**: Gabriel (business side) + Daniel (client/ads management)
- **Clients**: 5 tattoo artists
- **Revenue**: R$8,500 MRR (R$102,000 ARR projected)

### Current Structure
- **Legal Entity**: Mother's MEI (Microempreendedor Individual)
- **Tax Regime**: Simplified Brazilian tax system
- **Revenue Limit**: R$81,000 annually (R$6,750 monthly)
- **Current Status**: **Exceeding limit** (R$8,500 > R$6,750)

- **Integration**: Currently supplements German salary in overall finances

### MEI Compliance Issue
```
MEI Revenue Limit: R$81,000 annually (R$6,750 monthly)
Current Revenue: R$8,500 monthly (R$102,000 annually)
Excess: R$1,750 monthly (R$21,000 annually) OVER LIMIT
```

**Risk**: Operating above MEI limits violates Brazilian tax regulations and requires migration to different tax regime (Simples Nacional, Lucro Presumido, or Lucro Real).
