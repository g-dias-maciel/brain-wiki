## 📋 Document Overview

This is the German Tax Code (Abgabenordnung) for 2026, providing the legal framework for taxation in Germany. The document contains regulations covering income tax, corporate tax, trade tax, VAT, and other tax-related matters.

   - Tax returns and assessments
   - Appeals and objections
   - Tax audits
