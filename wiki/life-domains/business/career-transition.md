## Why B2B Contracting?

### Cost Efficiency for Employers
```
Employer Cost for €70k Gross Employee:
- Salary: €70,000
- Employer Social Contributions: ~€17,500-21,000 (25-30%)
- Total: ~€87,500-91,000

Employer Cost for €75k B2B Contractor:
- Contract: €75,000
- No social contributions
- Total: €75,000 (€12,500-16,000 SAVINGS)
```

### Personal Benefits
1. **Higher Net Income**: Equivalent to €70k gross salary but with better tax treatment
2. **Tax Optimization**: Wyoming LLC + V0050 exemption + Reverse Charge VAT
3. **Business Expense Deductions**: Margin interest as deductible business expense
4. **Flexibility**: Control over work arrangements and business structure

## Legal Framework (Germany 2026)

### V0050 Exemption
- **Law**: § 6 Abs. 1a SGB VI
- **Benefit**: 3-year exemption from German Social Security (pension insurance)
- **Condition**: Working for single main client as contractor
- **Application**: Requires proper documentation and compliance

### VAT Strategy
- **Wyoming LLC**: Bills German GmbHs without VAT
- **Reverse Charge**: Client liable for VAT locally (Art. 196 EU VAT Directive)
- **Efficiency**: Eliminates VAT complexity for international contractor

### Entity Structure
- **Primary Entity**: Wyoming LLC (tax-efficient, flexible)
- **German Presence**: No permanent establishment required for pure contracting
- **Banking**: Business accounts for LLC operations
