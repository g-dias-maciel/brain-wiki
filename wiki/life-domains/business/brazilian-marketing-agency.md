## Legal & Compliance Status

### Current Structure: MEI (Microempreendedor Individual)
- **Holder**: Mother's name
- **Tax Regime**: Simplified Brazilian tax system
- **Monthly Fee**: Fixed amount regardless of revenue
- **Services Allowed**: Limited to specific service codes
- **Employee Restriction**: Cannot have formal employees

### Compliance Issues
1. **Revenue Exceedance**: R$8,500 MRR > R$6,750 MEI limit
2. **Legal Risk**: Operating above limits violates Brazilian tax law
3. **Penalties**: Potential fines and back taxes
4. **Solution Required**: Migrate to proper business entity

### Migration Options
| Entity Type | Tax Regime | Revenue Limit | Employee Limit | Complexity |
|-------------|------------|---------------|----------------|------------|
| **MEI** | Simples Nacional | R$81k/year | 0 | Low |
| **EI** | Simples Nacional | R$4.8M/year | Unlimited | Medium |
| **EIRELI** | Simples/Lucro | No limit | Unlimited | High |
| **LTDA** | Simples/Lucro | No limit | Unlimited | High |

**Recommended**: EIRELI or LTDA for unlimited growth potential.
