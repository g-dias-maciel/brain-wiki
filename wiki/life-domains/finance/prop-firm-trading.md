## ⚠️ Risk Management

### Trading Risks
1. **Market Risk**: Exposure to market volatility and downturns
2. **Capital Risk**: Potential loss of trading capital
3. **Psychological Risk**: Emotional decision-making under pressure
4. **Strategy Risk**: Ineffective or outdated trading strategies

### Mitigation Strategies
- **Position Sizing**: Appropriate capital allocation per trade
- **Stop Losses**: Automated risk management protocols
- **Diversification**: Multiple strategies and timeframes
