## ⚙️ Operational Framework

### Data Collection Protocol
1. **Frequency**: Daily transaction recording, weekly categorization, monthly review
2. **Sources**: Bank accounts, credit cards, business accounts, investment accounts
3. **Categorization**: Standardized categories for consistency
4. **Validation**: Monthly reconciliation with statements

### Update & Review Cycle
```
Daily: Record transactions
Weekly: Categorize and review spending
Monthly: Comprehensive review and analysis
Quarterly: Strategy adjustment based on trends
Annually: Tax preparation and annual planning
```

### Quality Standards
1. **Completeness**: All financial transactions captured
2. **Accuracy**: Correct amounts and categorization
3. **Timeliness**: Current data for decision-making
4. **Consistency**: Standardized format across all data
